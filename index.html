AMSys

Monitoring athletes is critical for high performance and it has never been easier than with our product. Athletes are issued questionnaires digitally and placed into teams base on event type.



Coaches and teams are connected through a simple, vibrant dashboard view to increase collaboration, communication, and ultimately performance.



It is as easy as the simple steps shown below to increase an athlete's performance.


Data Gathering

The system gathers data on athletes including their height, weight and personal best. Information is also gathered on athletes from the daily questionnaire.

Analysis

All the athlete data is combined and an algorithm is used to make a prediction and recommendation. The system also calculates the athletes rate of percieved exertion and growth rate based on their previous running times.

Predictions and Recommendations

The system enables coaches to track daily progress of atletes, view predictions of future running times for an athlete and indentify their areas of strengths and weaknesses. This allows for schedules and training sessions that are tailored to the athlete in order to increase overall performance.

Coaches relax, We do the hard work

Making it easier for coaches and respective professionals to see all athletes' data and statistics in a secure manner allowing them to issue recommendations and performance advice with our software.
